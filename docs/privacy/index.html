SipCheck Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 30, 2026

SipCheck ("the app", "we") is a beer-tracking app. This policy explains what data the app handles and how. SipCheck has no user accounts and we do not operate servers that store your personal data — your data lives on your device and in your own iCloud account.

What the app stores

When you use SipCheck, the app stores the information you create: beers you add, ratings, tasting notes, scan history, journal entries, and any photos you attach. This data is saved locally on your device and, if you are signed in to iCloud, synced to your private iCloud database via Apple CloudKit so it is available on your other devices.

Your iCloud data is controlled by your Apple ID and Apple's iCloud privacy protections. The developer of SipCheck cannot see or access the contents of your private iCloud data.

Camera and photos

SipCheck uses the camera only when you choose to scan a beer label or attach a photo. Photos you capture are stored on your device and in your private iCloud; they are not shared with the developer.

AI label recognition

When you scan a beer, the app sends the label image and/or extracted text to third-party AI services — OpenAI, and optionally Google (Gemini) — solely to identify the beer and generate a recommendation. These requests do not include your name, account, or contact information. Use of those services is subject to their own privacy policies (OpenAI, Google).

What we do not do

SipCheck does not show ads, does not include third-party advertising or tracking SDKs, and does not sell your data. We do not collect analytics that identify you.

Deleting your data

You can delete individual items in the app, or use "Clear All Data" in Settings to remove everything. Deletions sync to your iCloud and remove the corresponding data there. Uninstalling the app removes its local data from that device.

Age

SipCheck is intended for users of legal drinking age and is rated 17+. It is not directed at children.

Changes

We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected on this page with a new "last updated" date.
